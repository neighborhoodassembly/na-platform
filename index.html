Neighborhood Assembly

We can work together to create a better future!

Neighborhood Assembly is a free, community-focused platform that makes it easy for neighbors to come together and make decisions online, securely and in real time. Whether you're organizing a local vote on shared spaces, budget changes, safety improvements, or community events, our tool empowers residents and leaders to engage in meaningful, transparent decision-making. Designed with simplicity and inclusion in mind, we help communities strengthen their voice — one vote at a time.

Because decisions belong to the people who live them. Neighborhood Assembly makes it easy, fair, and open for everyone to participate — anytime, anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Neighborhood Assembly is a free digital platform that allows local communities, neighborhood associations, and civic groups to run secure and transparent online votes. Our goal is to make community decision-making more inclusive and accessible.

Yes, Neighborhood Assembly is completely free for end users. We believe in making democratic tools accessible to all communities, regardless of their budget.

We use professional-grade authentication tools, identity verification, IP tracking, and HTTPS encryption to protect against fraud and unauthorized access. Each vote is logged securely and can be audited by administrators.

Each community has one or more designated moderators who can create votes, manage members, and view results. These roles can be assigned by the initial organizer or transferred over time.

Results are shown in real time and stored securely. Administrators can view detailed reports, and communities can choose whether to show results publicly or keep voter identities private while still maintaining transparency.
